Cheeseboat Receipt

Cheeseboat is a very popular Georgian dish

The fillings of Khachapuri vary with the area. The original recipe, the so-called imeruli, consisted cheese only

However, the other recipe used mostly especially in western part of Georgia, the so-called Cheeseboat, uses different shape of dough and egg

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • water
  • cheese
  • butter

Steps:

  1. Roll out the dough
  2. Give it a shape
  3. Put some cheese in there
  4. Cook for 20 minutes
  5. Eat
